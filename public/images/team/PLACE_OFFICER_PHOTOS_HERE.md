### Step 1: Prepare the Photo
1. Get a professional headshot of the officer
2. Crop to **square format** (1:1 ratio) - this looks best on the cards
3. Compress if needed (keep under 2MB)
4. Good lighting and plain background work best

### Step 2: Name the File
Use this format: `firstname-lastname.jpg`

**Examples:**
- `john-smith.jpg`
- `sarah-johnson.jpg`
- `alex-rodriguez.jpg`

**Important:**
- Use **lowercase** letters
- Use **hyphens** (not spaces or underscores)
- Accepted formats: `.jpg`, `.jpeg`, `.png`

### Step 3: Upload Here
Just drag and drop the photo into this folder!

### Step 4: Update team.ts
Go to `/src/content/data/team.ts` and add the officer's information.

**See the PRESIDENT_GUIDE.md in the root folder for detailed instructions!**
